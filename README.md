# 🫀 CardioCase AI

## Your AI-Powered Cardiology Study Companion

CardioCase AI is an AI-powered educational web application designed for BS Cardiology students, medical students, and healthcare learners.

The app helps students understand cardiology concepts, practice clinical reasoning, prepare for viva examinations, generate MCQs, and revise lecture materials with the help of Artificial Intelligence.

---

# 🎯 Problem It Solves

Cardiology students often face challenges such as:

- Understanding complex cardiovascular diseases
- Practicing clinical case reasoning
- Preparing for viva examinations
- Revising large lecture notes
- Testing knowledge before exams

CardioCase AI provides an interactive AI learning companion that makes cardiology study more effective, structured, and engaging.

# ✨ Features

## 🩺 AI Clinical Case Assistant

Students can enter symptoms and explore educational clinical scenarios.

Features:
- Clinical case generation
- Possible differential diagnoses
- Suggested investigations
- Learning points
- Clinical red flags

---

## 📚 AI Topic Explainer

Explains cardiology topics in a student-friendly way.

Provides:

- Definition
- Causes
- Symptoms
- Diagnosis overview
- Management overview
- Mnemonics

---

## 📝 AI MCQ Generator

Helps students prepare for exams by generating:

- Multiple-choice questions
- Correct answers
- Explanations
- Knowledge testing practice

---

## 📄 AI Notes Summarizer

Students can add lecture notes and generate:

- Short summaries
- Important key points
- Exam preparation tips

---

## 🎤 Viva Practice Assistant

Helps students prepare for oral examinations.

Features:

- Generates viva questions
- Allows students to practice answers
- Provides educational feedback

---

## 👤 Student Profile

Tracks learning activities including:

- Learning progress
- Completed quizzes
- Study statistics

---

# 🤖 AI Feature

CardioCase AI uses Gemini AI to provide an educational cardiology assistant.

The AI helps students:

- Learn cardiovascular concepts
- Practice clinical reasoning
- Generate study materials
- Prepare for examinations
- Improve understanding of medical topics
