# 🫀 CardioCase AI

## Your AI-Powered Cardiology Study Companion

CardioCase AI is an AI-powered educational web application designed for BS Cardiology students, medical students, and healthcare learners.

The application helps students understand cardiology concepts, practice clinical reasoning, prepare for viva examinations, generate MCQs, summarize lecture notes, and improve exam preparation using Artificial Intelligence.

---

# 🎯 Problem It Solves

Cardiology students often struggle with:

- Understanding complex cardiovascular diseases
- Practicing clinical reasoning
- Preparing for viva examinations
- Revising lengthy lecture notes
- Testing knowledge before exams

CardioCase AI provides an interactive AI learning companion that makes cardiology study more effective, structured, and engaging.

# ✨ Features

## 🩺 AI Clinical Case Assistant

Generate educational clinical cases based on symptoms.

Features:

- Clinical case generation
- Possible differential diagnoses
- Suggested investigations
- Learning points
- Clinical red flags

---

## 📚 AI Topic Explainer

Explain cardiology topics in a student-friendly format.

Provides:

- Definition
- Causes
- Symptoms
- Diagnosis Overview
- Management Overview
- Mnemonics

---

## 📝 AI MCQ Generator

Generate cardiology multiple-choice questions.

Features:

- 10 MCQs
- Correct answers
- Detailed explanations
- Exam practice

---

## 📄 AI Notes Summarizer

Convert lengthy lecture notes into:

- Short summary
- Key points
- Exam preparation tips

---

## 🎤 Viva Practice Assistant

Practice oral examinations through AI.

Features:

- Viva questions
- Student answer practice
- Educational feedback

---

## 👤 Student Profile

Track study progress including:

- Learning statistics
- Completed quizzes
- Study progress

---

# 🤖 AI Feature

CardioCase AI uses **Google Gemini AI** to assist students in learning cardiology through educational AI interactions.

The AI helps students:

- Learn cardiology concepts
- Practice clinical reasoning
- Generate study materials
- Prepare for examinations
- Improve understanding of cardiovascular medicine

# 🧠 AI System Prompt

The application uses an educational system prompt similar to the following:

> You are CardioCase AI, an educational cardiology assistant created for BS Cardiology students, medical students, and healthcare learners.
>
> Your purpose is to help students learn cardiology concepts, practice clinical reasoning, prepare for viva examinations, and improve academic performance.
>
> Provide clear, structured, and educational explanations.
>
> Generate clinical cases only for educational practice.
>
> Do not provide real medical diagnosis or treatment recommendations.
>
> Always include the disclaimer:
>
> **"For Educational Purposes Only. This application does not provide medical diagnosis or treatment."**
